Welcome to Logic Coffee

Discover the world of coffee with us.

About Logic Coffee

Logic Coffee is a student-run organization at the university that focuses on the understanding and appreciation of coffee. We are passionate about sharing our knowledge and experiences with the community.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore the world of coffee, from its history and cultivation to its diverse flavors and brewing methods.

Our Activities

Coffee Tasting

We organize regular coffee tasting events where participants can explore different coffee origins, roast profiles, and brewing techniques.

Barista Workshop

Our barista workshops teach students the art of making specialty coffee drinks, from espresso-based beverages to manual brew methods.

Coffee Cupping

We host coffee cupping sessions where participants can learn to evaluate the aroma, flavor, and overall quality of different coffee samples.

Our Divisions

Research and Development

Our R&D division explores new coffee varieties, processing methods, and brewing techniques to expand our knowledge and offer innovative products.

Marketing and Events

The marketing and events division is responsible for promoting our activities, organizing workshops and coffee-related events, and engaging with the community.

Operations and Logistics

The operations and logistics division ensures the smooth running of our organization, handling procurement, inventory management, and distribution of our coffee products.

Questions & Answers

What is Logic Coffee?

Logic Coffee is a student-run organization at the university that focuses on the understanding and appreciation of coffee. We are passionate about sharing our knowledge and experiences with the community.

What are the benefits of joining Logic Coffee?

By joining Logic Coffee, you'll have the opportunity to explore the world of coffee, learn from experienced baristas, and connect with a community of coffee enthusiasts. Our events and workshops provide a platform for personal growth and skill development.

How can I get involved with Logic Coffee?

There are several ways to get involved with Logic Coffee. You can attend our events, volunteer for our activities, or apply to join one of our divisions. Visit our website or contact us for more information on how to get started.

Do you offer any internships or job opportunities?

Yes, we do offer internship opportunities for students interested in gaining practical experience in the coffee industry. We also occasionally post job openings for various positions within our organization. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for the latest updates.
